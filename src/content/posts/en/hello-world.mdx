After ten years of shipping backend systems without writing much about it, I'm finally starting a place to share the thinking behind the work. Expect notes on distributed systems, event-driven architecture, infrastructure-as-code, observability — and occasional detours into home brewing and Arduino.

Posts will be in **English by default**, with **Portuguese** translations for anything that might be useful to the BR engineering community. Use the language switcher at the top-right to flip between them.

If you'd like to get in touch, the [Contact](/contact) page has the channels I check. Thanks for reading.
